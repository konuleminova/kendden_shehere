The project of the State Agro Trade Company “Kendden Shehere” is aimed at providing support to owners of small and medium-sized farms and local food products producers in bringing their products to market.

The online trading platform “Kendden Shehere” is aimed at providing customers with high-quality service, as well as covering their food needs quickly and comfortably. By placing an order online, you can buy natural, healthy local products without leaving your home or office.

The State Agro Trade Company LLC supports the concept of agricultural policy implemented by the state and ensures uninterrupted, unimpeded and competitive export of agricultural products, produced in the country, to the local and foreign markets in order to achieve the development of agricultural producers and ensure the growth of agricultural production, thereby contributing to the development of the country's economy.
