Dear customer!
You can easily order any product available at the “Kendden Shehere” shop (hereinafter - the “Shop”), with delivery to any place in Baku.

𝐃𝐄𝐋𝐈𝐕𝐄𝐑𝐘 𝐂𝐎𝐒𝐓𝐒
Delivery is carried out within Baku.

FREE delivery on all orders over 20 AZN within the territories near 28 May, Icheri Sheher, Khatai and Ganjlik metro stations. Delivery to the areas near the other metro stations, including in the Sabail District, is 4 AZN

Delivery to Baku villages and other districts is 7 AZN.

xOn the occasion of the launch of the site, delivery throughout the city is FREE, until May 5, 2019.

𝐄𝐒𝐓𝐈𝐌𝐀𝐓𝐄𝐃 𝐃𝐄𝐋𝐈𝐕𝐄𝐑𝐘 𝐓𝐈𝐌𝐄
You can choose one of two delivery options for your order:

Standard delivery and express delivery.

Standard delivery is carried out twice a day from 11:30 to 13:00 and from 19:00 to 20:30.

You can choose one of these two-time intervals, after adding the goods to the basket and placing an order. The order must be placed no later than 11:00, to ensure its delivery from 11:30 to 13:00, and no later than 18:30 for its delivery from 19:00 to 20:30.

Express delivery: If you need the ordered goods as soon as possible, you can arrange express delivery for an additional cost - 2 AZN. If you choose express delivery, your order will be delivered within 30-60 minutes, depending on the delivery location.

𝐏𝐀𝐘𝐌𝐄𝐍𝐓
You can pay for the order and delivery by card or on arrival (in cash).

If you decide to pay with a card, you need to pass from the website’s payment page to the Kapital Bank’s payment page and make a secure payment using Visa or MasterCard cards.

If you decide to pay on arrival, you need to pay the amount indicated in the receipt we provide, in cash to the employee who delivered the order.
